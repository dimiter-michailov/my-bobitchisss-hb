Happy Birthday, María!

My bobitchis,

I love you more than grilled cheese.

Even in the Netherlands, you bring green trees.

If you practice law then help me escape legal fees.


My bobitkiss,

you almost have two degrees.

I hope that tutor gets stung by a swarm of bees,

but it will be fine because you will write a genial thesis.


My bonita,

Your face tastes like a pitta.

You watch too much tiktok when you are abborida.

If you don’t like this poem, I will make you again a fajita.


My bobitka,

I really like you taste in musíca.

Next time I will write you a song on the harmonica,

and instead of words I will tell you how much I love you with 

a melodía.
